## Data-Driven Astronomy 🚀

This repository contains research projects I completed during the **Astronomy & Astrophysics Summer School – 2025** organized by the **India Space Academy (ISA)**.  
The focus is on applying **data science techniques** to real astronomical datasets, combining astrophysical theory with computational methods.  

### 🔬 Projects Included
1. **Dynamical Mass of a Star Cluster**  
   - Estimated the dynamical mass using stellar velocity dispersion and cluster radius.  
   - Applied statistical methods to evaluate uncertainties and compared results with theoretical predictions.  

2. **Identifying Spectral Lines (JWST MIRI Data)**  
   - Processed mid-infrared spectra from the **James Webb Space Telescope (MIRI instrument)**.  
   - Identified key emission and absorption lines from stellar and interstellar sources.  
   - Interpreted physical properties of stars and surrounding medium based on detected lines.  

### 🛠️ Tools & Methods
- Python (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, SciPy, Astropy)  
- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing  
- Statistical Modeling & Error Analysis  
- Visualization of spectra and cluster dynamics  

### 🎯 Outcomes
- Hands-on application of **data science in astrophysics**.  
- Gained experience in **star cluster dynamics** and **spectral line identification**.  
- Strengthened skills in handling and analyzing real **astronomical datasets**.
